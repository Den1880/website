Summer Highlights: August at Den 1880

Community Newsletter · August 2025

It’s hard to believe August is already here! We hope you’re soaking up every bit of sunshine and enjoying the long days of summer. Things have been buzzing here at Den 1880, and we’ve loved welcoming so many new members into the space these past few weeks.

A Look Back: Music Trivia Madness

A huge shoutout to Host Wanted Productions and everyone who joined us for Music Trivia Night in July. You all brought the energy (and the deep-cut song knowledge). Congratulations to our winning team, Warmingtunes, your music skills seriously rocked.

Up Next: Member Summer BBQ

Summer fun continues with our Member BBQ on Wednesday, August 6th at 4:00 PM. We’re serving up delicious smoked bites from Lancaster Smokehouse, refreshing summer sangria, and plenty of good vibes, all on the house. Come hang out, play a few games, connect with fellow members, and make the most of summer on the patio.

Christmas in July: Holiday Party Bookings

If you’re starting to think about your team’s holiday party, now’s the perfect time to plan ahead. Members get a special discount when booking their holiday celebration with us at Den 1880, but dates are going fast. Email events@den1880.co to reserve your spot.

Fresh Eats from The Three

Good news for your weekday lunch routine: The Three Wellness now delivers to Den Monday through Friday. Place your order by 11:00 AM, include “Den Member” in the notes, and your meal will arrive at 12:00 PM in the Den Kitchen. Want it brought right to your desk? Just add “Desk” to your order notes, and lunch is served.

Planning a Team Offsite?

From team retreats to strategy sessions, Den 1880 makes hosting your next offsite simple and seamless. With flexible layouts, local catering options, and hands-on event support, we’ll help make your day both productive and enjoyable. Email hello@den1880.co to learn more.

Move, Stretch, and Flow

Pilates will be taking a short break on August 4th and 11th, but Yoga with Bettina continues every Wednesday at 8:15 AM in the Theatre. Start your morning with a beginner-friendly flow and leave feeling grounded and refreshed.That’s it for now, but as always, there’s lots happening behind the scenes at Den 1880. Stay tuned for more updates, community spotlights, and upcoming events.

Here’s to a sunny, social, and inspiring August ahead. – Team Den
