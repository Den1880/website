The Teams Floor

An office without the overhead.

Twenty-plus private offices sit on Den 1880's dedicated Teams Floor — a secure, professional environment designed specifically for teams who want the feel of their own space without the cost and commitment of a commercial lease.

Skip the furniture order, the IT setup, the internet installation, and the three-year contract. Your office is ready the day you are.

Offices accommodate teams of 1 to 35, with lockable suites, sit-to-stand desks, and keyless entry. Every office pairs naturally with a Team plan — your suite is your HQ, the plan covers the gym, wellness, and everything else in the building.
