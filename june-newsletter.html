June at Den 1880: Big Events & Our Magazine Debut

Community Newsletter · June 2025

Summer has officially arrived at Den 1880, and there’s no better time to be part of our thriving coworking community in Uptown Waterloo.

Den 1880 featured on Toque Magazine’s Design Issue Cover

We’re incredibly proud to share that Den 1880 is on the cover of Toque Magazine’s Design Issue! The feature includes an in-depth interview with our COO, Jacklyn Warmington, spotlighting the intentional design behind our coworking space and the role it plays in building community. Stop by the kitchen to pick up your copy and join us at Member Happy Hour to celebrate.

New Outdoor Workspaces — Now Open

We’ve added new shaded outdoor tables and seating areas so members can enjoy the summer breeze while they work. Whether you're taking a meeting outside or soaking up some mid-day sun, our expanded office space helps make the workday a little brighter.

Upcoming Events at Den 1880

From social gatherings to creative workshops and corporate events, our calendar is packed with ways to connect with fellow members and experience something new.

Member Happy Hour

Thursday, June 5 | 4–6PM | Den Kitchen Join your fellow coworkers for our monthly Member Happy Hour — a casual gathering featuring complimentary drinks and snacks in the Den Kitchen. It’s a great way to meet new faces in the coworking community and unwind after work. Guests are welcome!

Comedy Den

Doors at 7PM | Show at 8PM Comedy Den is back with a stellar night of laughs, powered by Rusty Nail Comedy. This month’s headliner is the incredible Martha Chaves. Choose from VIP and Date Night packages, or keep it casual with general admission. Den members get 15% off with an exclusive code found in your member portal.

Member Line Dancing Class

Friday, June 6 | 7–10PM Get ready to two-step! This lively class is perfect for all experience levels — your ticket includes a guided session, one drink, and snacks. Bring a guest and enjoy one of the most fun events we’ve hosted in our Uptown Waterloo space.

Weddings & Corporate Events at Den

Our stunning coworking space also transforms into an unforgettable venue for weddings and corporate events. With curated catering, full bar service, live music options, and even a photo booth, Den 1880 is redefining what’s possible in a multi-functional coworking and office space. Looking for a unique venue for a special event in Waterloo? Contact events@den1880.co

Vines & Wines

Friday, June 27 | Doors 6:30PM | Event 7PM This crowd favourite is back! Create a floral arrangement with Le Jardin, followed by a wine tasting led by Robert Miller from The Vine Agency. It’s the perfect way to wind down your week — and a great opportunity to learn something new!

Decades of Sound: Music Trivia

Tuesday, July 15 | 7–10PM We're bringing the music and the memories at this Waterloo coworking event in partnership with Host Wanted Productions. Form your trivia dream team and take on decades of pop, rock, disco, and more. Great for team bonding or just a night of fun! Den members get 15% off with exclusive code found in your member portal.

Why Den 1880

Whether you're seeking a beautiful office space, a flexible desk, a collaborative coworking environment, or a vibrant hub for corporate events and social gatherings, Den 1880 offers it all in the heart of Uptown Waterloo.

We’re more than just a place to work. We’re a Waterloo coworking space built on connection, creativity, and community. Thank you for being part of it — and if you’re new here, we can’t wait to welcome you.

– Team Den
