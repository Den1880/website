October at Den 1880: Music, Community and Connections

Community Newsletter · October 2025

The first day of October is here, and our community at Den 1880 is ready for an exciting month ahead. September was full of energy, connection, and fun as we welcomed members back to the office with Welcome Week. From Pizza Day and Member Happy Hour to Free Day Pass Friday and Comedy Den, our coworking space was buzzing with laughter, collaboration, and good vibes.

Upcoming Events: Secret Concert

We’re kicking October off with a Secret Concert presented by Good Co. Productions on Thursday, October 2nd. The Den Theatre will transform into an intimate listening room with two Canadian touring acts guaranteed to impress. Guests won’t know who’s performing until they arrive, making it a night full of surprises!

Den Members can save $5 on tickets, send us a message for your exclusive discount code. Don’t miss this unique opportunity to enjoy music, connect with your coworking community, and bring friends along.

Halloween Happy Hour | Wednesday, October 15

Add some fall fun to your midweek with our Halloween-themed Member Happy Hour. Join us at 5 PM in the Kitchen for seasonal drinks, light bites, and pumpkin carving. Show off your creativity in our pumpkin competition, meet new faces, and reconnect with fellow members in our coworking space.

Weekly Community Perks

At Den 1880, our coworking community isn’t just about work — it’s about lifestyle and well-being:

  • Poppy’s Bagel Day: Complimentary bagels every Tuesday at 9 AM, courtesy of our Uptown neighbours, available on a first-come, first-served basis in the Kitchen.
  • Weekly Fitness Classes: All classes are free for members and beginner-friendly. Mats provided, guests can join for $15.
  • Pilates with Liv | Mondays at 4 PM
  • Yoga with Bettina | Wednesdays at 4 PM in the Theatre

Sports and Movie Screenings

We’ve heard your requests — Den 1880 members can now enjoy sporting events on our big screen. Want to catch a specific game? Let us know, and we’ll make it happen.

Den 1880 is more than just an office space — it’s a thriving coworking community where members collaborate, socialize, and grow together. From weekly events and wellness classes to special concerts and themed happy hours, October promises a month full of connection, creativity, and shared experiences.

Here’s to making fall a season to remember at Den 1880. – Team Den
