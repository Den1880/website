July at Den 1880: Summer Celebrations, New Faces & Exciting Updates

Community Newsletter · July 2025

Summer has officially arrived, and things are heating up at Den 1880, your go-to coworking space in Uptown Waterloo. From heartfelt goodbyes to exciting hellos, June was packed with milestones, laughter, and memorable events — and we’re just getting started.

Farewell to a Team Member

This month, we said a bittersweet goodbye to Sonvin, who’s heading off to an exciting new chapter in Toronto. As one of Den’s earliest team members, Sonvin has played an instrumental role in shaping our coworking community. Her warmth, energy, and tireless dedication have left a lasting mark — and she’ll always be part of the Den family. Thank you for everything, Sonvin. We’ll miss you dearly!

Welcoming New Teams to the Den Community

June also brought fresh energy to our office spaces with the arrival of two new teams on our dedicated Teams Floor: Blackstone Energy and Cast Insights. We’re thrilled to have them join our growing community of entrepreneurs and professionals here in Uptown Waterloo.

Whether you're a solo founder or a growing business, Den offers everything from shared office space to private team offices, with all the perks of a professional setup — and none of the hassle.

A Night of Laughs: Comedy Den Recap

A big thank you to everyone who joined us for Comedy Den, powered by Rusty Nail Comedy. With headliner Adam Christie and featured act Chris Robinson, the night brought huge laughs and good vibes all around. If you missed it, don’t worry — Comedy Den will be back this September with more top-tier acts.

Say “I Do” at Den 1880

We’ve had the joy of hosting some truly beautiful weddings this season! Den 1880 is now partnering with top local vendors to offer all-inclusive wedding packages in a truly unique setting. From curated catering and a full-service bar to custom photo booths and live music, every detail is thoughtfully handled.

If you're looking for a non-traditional wedding venue in Waterloo, our space blends historic charm with modern design — perfect for couples who want something a little different.

Know someone getting married? Email paige@den1880.co to learn more about our intimate wedding venue options in Uptown Waterloo.

Why Choose Den 1880 for Work & Events?

Den 1880 isn’t just a place to work — it’s a space where community, creativity, and connection thrive. Whether you're here for a day pass, a dedicated desk, or hosting a corporate event in Waterloo, Den offers a unique blend of style, function, and flexibility.

We’re proud to provide coworking space in Waterloo for:

  • Remote workers and freelancers
  • Small teams and startups
  • Corporate offsites and workshops
  • Creative professionals and content creators

With thoughtful amenities, curated events, and an engaging community, Den 1880 makes it easy to love where you work.

Stay Connected

Keep an eye out for more exciting summer events, member perks, and community news coming your way soon.

From all of us at Den 1880 — happy summer! - Team Den
