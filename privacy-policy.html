Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Den 1880 ("we," "us," "our") operates the website den1880.co. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit the site or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. Den 1880 is located at 14 Erb St. West, Uptown Waterloo, Ontario, Canada N2L 1S7. You can reach us at hello@den1880.co or (519) 783-1505.

Information we collect

Information you give us. When you fill out a form on our site, book a tour, ask about an event, apply for a membership or business mailbox, or email us, we collect the details you provide. That usually means your name, email address, phone number, and whatever you write in your message.

Information collected automatically. When you browse the site, we and our analytics and advertising partners automatically collect standard technical data: your device and browser type, IP address, the pages you view, how you got to the site, and how you interact with it. This is collected through cookies and similar technologies (see below).

Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies and pixels to understand how people use the site and to measure and improve our advertising. The tools we currently run are:

  • Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity — to see how visitors use the site (pages, traffic sources, on-page behavior, heatmaps, and session recordings that exclude sensitive input).
  • Google Ads, Microsoft (Bing) Ads, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), TikTok, and LinkedIn advertising pixels — to measure which ads lead to actions like tour bookings and signups, and to show relevant ads to people who've visited the site.

These partners process the data on our behalf and under their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information.

How we use your information

We use the information to respond to your inquiries, provide tours, memberships, event bookings, and other services you ask about, operate and improve the website, measure and improve our marketing, and meet our legal obligations.

How your information is shared

We share information with the analytics and advertising providers listed above, and with service providers who help us run the business (for example, our booking and membership platform). We may also disclose information if required by law. We don't sell your personal information.

Your choices and rights

You can control cookies through your browser settings, and you can opt out of personalized advertising through each platform's ad settings (for example, Google Ads Settings and the Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Microsoft ad preference controls). You can also opt out of Google Analytics using Google's browser add-on.

If you're in Canada, you have rights under PIPEDA to access the personal information we hold about you and to ask us to correct it. To make a request, email us at hello@den1880.co and we'll respond within a reasonable time.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as we need it for the purposes described here or as required by law, then delete or anonymize it.

Third-party links

Our site links to other services (such as our booking and membership platform on den1880.optixapp.com). Those services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to read them. We're not responsible for the practices of sites we don't operate.

Children

The site is intended for adults and isn't directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll change the "Last updated" date at the top. Significant changes will be made clear on this page.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Email hello@den1880.co or call (519) 783-1505, or write to us at 14 Erb St. West, Uptown Waterloo, Ontario, Canada N2L 1S7.
