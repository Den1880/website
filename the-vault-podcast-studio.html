About The Vault

Where great content gets made.

The Vault is housed inside Den 1880's original 1900s bank vault — a space with 12-inch steel-reinforced walls that provides natural soundproofing unlike any purpose-built studio. The history inside these walls is as compelling as the content recorded here.

Three track-mounted cameras capture every angle automatically. Four Shure microphones, broadcast-grade lighting, and a Frame TV branding display mean your production looks polished from the first session — no crew required.

Toronto studios of this calibre average $100/hr. The Vault delivers the same quality at boutique rates, ten minutes from downtown Kitchener and steps from the LRT.
